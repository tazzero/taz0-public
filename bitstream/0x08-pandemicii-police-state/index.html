Published: Monday, Mar 30, 2020   |   2 minute read   |   Using 244 words

We talk with Arto Bendiken about the political reactions to the ongoing pandemic and their long term effects on: Economy, free speech, mass gatherings, biodefense, cash, infection control, and identity.
